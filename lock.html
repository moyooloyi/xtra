Goodbye to unnecessary spending with Xtra Lock

Developing the habit of saving is a good thing, but when you withdraw your savings at every opportunity you get, that is where there is a concern.

The skill of not touching your savings at all is different for everyone. It can be likened to looking at your favorite meal without eating it, yes we get it, that is definitely not an easy task. Nevertheless, it is absolutely acheivable. You can take that xtra step by setting aside a particular amount of money and locking it on Xtra Lock.

You need to save for that dream car, rent, house, phone, etc and you want to avoid temptation of spending the money? Xtra Lock gives you the opportunity for that. You also get to enjoy 10% interest on the money.

When you put a particular amount in your Xtra Lock account, the minimum withdrawal date is 6 months. Like the previous example given, you only get to smell your favorite food but you won't be able to eat it.

Open a Xtra Lock account with these easy steps

Select Payment Option

There are different options you can save. You can either use your card or through your bank

State the amount you want to lock

Set aside your rent, car etc money little by little so you can acheive your goal at the end of the day

Set a withdrawal date

The minimum withdrawal date is 6 months. Set the date that would be comfortable for you, it can be a year or more

Your money is locked

Your money is secured and locked till the withdrawal date that has been set
