What is Xtra?

Xtra is an online savings and investment platform. We make savings easy for our customers and help them work towards acheiving their financial goal.

Do i get interests for my savings?

You get 3.2% interest for your savings and the interest is added to your Xtra Flex account.

How frequent am i allowed to withdraw my savings?

A withdrawal date would be set when you can withdraw your savings without any charges. If you withdraw before your withdrawal date, an amount would be used for charges. This helps to reduce frequent withdrawals so you can develop a goood saving habit.

Is there a savings plan?

You can save daily, weekly or monthly, depending on what suits you. You can also set an automatic withdrawal date and time, few hours before the amount is deducted, you will be notified. This feature can be disabled anytime.

What investment plans do you offer?

In Xtra, we have Crypto & Blockchain Investments, Real Estate Investments, Agriculture and Livestock Investments, Bonds and Stocks Investments, Stanbic IBTC Equity Fund, PayVest etc. Choose the one that works best for you.
