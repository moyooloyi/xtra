Saving is income not spent, or deferred consumption. Saving is closely related to physical investment, in that the former provides a source of funds for the latter. Saving also involves reducing expenditures, such as recurring costs. In terms of personal finance, saving generally specifies low-risk preservation of money.

Xtra Saving is an e-wallet where your savings are deposited. You can save daily, weekly, monthly or per annum. Your Xtra account is linked to your local bank accounts for easy deposits and withdrawals.

On Xtra, you can set a date and time to be deducted from your account for your savings. There would be a reminder option to make it easier for you to remember the date and time set for the deduction. This feature can be disabled anytime.

There is also an option for quick deposits. You can save money automatically and within few minutes, the money is reflected in your e-wallet.
